About Us

The Aga Khan University Hospital, Nairobi (AKUH) is a premier healthcare institution dedicated to providing high-quality medical services in East Africa. With a commitment to excellence, we offer advanced healthcare driven by our values of compassion, respect, and integrity.

Our Services

  • Emergency Services: 24/7 emergency care for critical conditions.
  • Inpatient Services: Comprehensive care for hospitalized patients.
  • Outpatient Services: Specialized consultations and follow-up treatments.
  • Surgical Services: Advanced surgical procedures with highly trained specialists.
  • Maternity Services: Prenatal care, labor, delivery, and postpartum support.
  • Diagnostics: State-of-the-art imaging and laboratory services.

Specialties

Cardiology

Expert care for heart-related conditions, including diagnostic tests and treatments.

Oncology

Comprehensive cancer care, including chemotherapy, radiotherapy, and surgical options.

Pediatrics

Healthcare services tailored for infants, children, and adolescents.

Orthopedics

Treatment for musculoskeletal issues, including injuries and joint replacements.

Neurology

Diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders affecting the brain and nervous system.
